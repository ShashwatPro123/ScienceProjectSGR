BIG BANG

Around fourteen billion years ago, the universe was made out od nothing for uknown reasons. Infinitely smaller than an atom to begin with, the universe expanded to a trillion kilometers under a second, Milky way was just a part of that, and this theory was called THE BIG BANG THEORY.



Birth Of The Solar System

An interstellar cloud formed after some time and then that broke into fragments which again formed a protostar which started collecting the nearby materials. The gas and the solids that came out formed the stars and the leftover materials formed the planets.
